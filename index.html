Madlen Bakery

We bake biscuits in all shapes, sizes and varieties for both corporate and private orders. No matter how big or small the order, you have the option to design your own bespoke / personalised biscuits. Onto which we can imprint your logo, brand values, special hashtags for an event or simply personalised messages for those special occasions in your life.

We also bake delicious cakes, fruit pies & brownies! We cater both to private parties as well as coffee shops, cafes, hotels, etc. who would like to order wholesale cakes or biscuits from us.
